Quizzy Privacy Policy

Thank you for using Quizzy! This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information we collect

When you use Quizzy, we may collect the following information:

  • Your name and email address (if you choose to create an account)
  • Your quiz scores and progress
  • Device information (such as your device type and operating system)
  • Log data (such as IP address and browser type)

We collect this information to provide and improve Quizzy, and to communicate with you about Quizzy updates and promotions. We do not collect any sensitive personal information such as your race, religion, political affiliation, or health.

2. How we use your information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve Quizzy
  • To communicate with you about Quizzy updates and promotions
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights
  • To personalize your experience with Quizzy

We do not share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data retention and security

We store your information securely in Firebase, which is a cloud-based database. We retain your information as long as necessary to provide and improve Quizzy, or as required by law. We take reasonable measures to protect your information against unauthorized access or disclosure, but no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

4. Children's privacy

Quizzy is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

5. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you by email or through Quizzy. Your continued use of Quizzy after any changes indicates your acceptance of the new policy.

6. Contact us

We take privacy very seriously and will do our best to address any concerns you may have.
